Tax Services

Tax and VAT is a part of everyone's life and is something all clients must consider, whether they are a business or an individual.

Benchmark Accountants has the knowledge to support businesses in a variety of sectors and works closely with clients to plan for the present and the future. Whilst minimising tax liabilities is important, we consider the wider picture and look at tax planning issues alongside commercial reality and the practicalities of life.

Our areas of expertise include:

  • VAT Returns
  • Corporate Tax
  • Annual Accounts
  • Book keeping Services
  • Personal Tax and Self Assessment
  • Company Secretarial Services
  • Payroll
  • PAYE

VAT Registration

Benchmark Accountants will take away the hassle of you having to worry about completing your tax return, provided you supply all the relevant information and documents to us within an appropriate timescale.

For VAT registration we can assist with:

  • Advising if it would be beneficial to register
  • Completion of the VAT registration forms and formalities
  • Advising on the best VAT schemes to use suitable to your business

In addition we can assist with:

  • Completion of VAT Returns or training on how to complete them
  • VAT planning
  • Attendance at VAT inspections

VAT Returns

Compliance with VAT is complex. Investigations by the VATman can be intense,it is therefore important that your VAT returns are completed correctly.

There are three methods we can use to assist in completion of VAT returns:

  • Let us do your book-keeping and we complete the return from there
  • Give us your books and we'll complete the return for you
  • Provide training on how to complete your return

We complete VAT returns in good time to ensure you avoid any penalties

Annual Accounts Preparation

We can:

  • Prepare accounts to help in completion of self-assessment tax returns
  • Produce financial statements to file at Companies House and abridged accounts to ensure the minimum amount of your financial information is made public

Accounts are prepared to agreed timescales and deadlines

But we can do more than just report the history of what's already happened:

  • Identify areas where we can assist in minimising your tax liability
  • Identify areas of the business that give you the most opportunity to make improvements
  • Use the accounts to help you measure where you are in meeting your goals and what actions you need to take

We take the time to explain your accounts to you so that you understand what is going on financially within your business, helping you to plan for the future
